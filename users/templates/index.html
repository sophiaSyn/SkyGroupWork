About Us

At SKY, we believe that a strong, motivated, and efficient team is the foundation of great work. The SKY Team Health Check portal is designed to provide insights into our team's overall well-being, performance, and collaboration.

Our goal is simple: help teams work better, together. By regularly assessing key factors like team morale, code quality, delivery efficiency, and process effectiveness, we can identify areas for growth and make meaningful improvements.
